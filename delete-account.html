Delete Your Account

DaengDaeng Diary | Account & Data Deletion Guide

You can delete your DaengDaeng Diary account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to request account deletion and what data will be removed.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the DaengDaeng Diary app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us at qkr7287@gmail.com. Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Data Type Action Timeframe
Account information (email, display name) Permanently deleted Immediately
Profile photo Permanently deleted Immediately
Diary entries and mood records Permanently deleted Immediately
Diary images Permanently deleted Within 30 days
Error/crash logs Automatically expired Within 90 days
Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your diary entries, images, and shared diary data will be permanently removed. If you are a diary owner, the shared diary will also be deleted for all members.
