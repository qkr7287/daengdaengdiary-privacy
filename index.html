Information How We Collect Required/Optional
Email address, display name Automatically collected via Google Sign-In Required
Profile photo Google account or user upload Optional
Diary entries, mood/emotion records User input Optional (during use)
Diary images User upload Optional

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, your data will be promptly removed. Exceptions may apply where retention is required by applicable law.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

You have the right to:
